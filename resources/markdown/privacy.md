## What we collect

Your name, email, delivery address and order history — the minimum needed to
fulfil your order and provide support. Payments are processed by our payment
provider; we never see or store your bank credentials.

## What we do with it

We use your details to fulfil orders, send order updates, and respond to
support requests. We don't sell your data, and we don't send marketing
without your consent.

## Cookies

We use only essential cookies (session and security). No tracking or
advertising cookies.

## Your rights

You can request a copy of your data or ask us to delete your account and
data at any time using the contact email in the footer.
