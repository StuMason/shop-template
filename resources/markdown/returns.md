# Returns & Cancellation

> **REPLACE BEFORE TRADING.** This is placeholder text shipped with the shop
> template, written for UK consumer law (Consumer Contracts Regulations 2013).

## Your right to cancel

You have the right to cancel your order within **14 days** of receiving your
goods, without giving any reason. To exercise it, contact us using the email
in the footer with your order number.

If you cancel, return the goods to us within 14 days of telling us. We'll
refund the full price plus standard delivery within 14 days of receiving the
goods back (or proof of postage). Return postage is your responsibility
unless the item is faulty.

## Faulty or wrong items

If something arrives damaged, faulty, or isn't what you ordered, contact us
and we'll make it right — replacement or full refund including return
postage. Your statutory rights under the Consumer Rights Act 2015 are
unaffected.

## How refunds are paid

Refunds are returned to the bank account you paid from.
